Statement about "Age-Insight"

In the beginning of Fall 2015, I developed and released a small Chrome extension called “Age-Insight”. The extension is a tiny program that calculates the age of a person and then it shows that calculation on their LinkedIn Profiles.

I developed this extension because sometimes, for no particular reason, I like to know how old is someone."Age-Insight" is simply a consequence of my own curiosity.

In October 16th, “Age-Insight” was featured on Product Hunt. After some hours of being featured many people started to address an issue with this tool.
“Age-Insight” could be easily abused and used by unscrupulous people to discriminate others.

I share those concerns and I certainly didn’t develop this tool to encourage such a blameworthy behavior. For this reason I have decided to SHUT DOWN AND STOP GIVING ANY KIND OF SUPPORT for “Age-Insight”.

If you’re one of the hundreds of users who downloaded “Age-Insight” (users who I’m sure have no intention of using this tool beyond their own curiosity), please be advised that by using “Age-Insight” you may be violating LinkedIn User’s Agreement and breaching your contract and/or interfering with your contractual relationship with LinkedIn.

For this reason I highly suggest you to uninstall / remove the official extension from your Chrome browser and avoid reinstalling any .CRX copy you may find online. I don't control any of those copies and they could have been modified to include malware.

I want to restate that effective today (October 18th, 2015):
1) I am STOPPING THE DISTRIBUTION, DEVELOPMENT AND SUPPORT of “Age-Insight”.
2) Any copy of "Age-Insight" you find online IS NOT DISTRIBUTED, DEVELOPED OR SUPPORTED BY ME.

Thanks to everyone,

Juan J. Ramirez
October 18th, 2015.

-How Does It Work-

Is not rocket science. We transform a simple calculation you're probably already doing,
into an auto-pilot and data informed insight that gets better overtime.

Semantic Analysis
of Profile Information

Age-Insight for LinkedIn does a semantic analysis of the available profile information (education + experience) to make an educated guess on a person's age. It also analizes the accuracy of the information and only shows a guess if there's enough data to compose an accurate calculation.

Powered by Data,
that Increases Accuracy

Age-Insight for LinkedIn, uses different datasets to cross-check and improve the accuracy of a guess.

The underlaying technology behind the extension fine-tunes the calculation by using every piece of available information (from current location to alma-mater, and even the person's name itself) and compares it against the available and growing datasets.
