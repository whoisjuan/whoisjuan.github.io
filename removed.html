REMIXER

Remixer is a a Human Computer Interaction project in which we explored the different uses of prototying techniques + novel interaction methodologies to come up with a sample product that helps users to mix cocktails. I created the designs of an app and co-created the companion shaker that helps user to mix cocktails. The project deliverable was a prototype of the actual shaker using a 3D-Printer and a Laser-cutter, as well as a real app with different cocktail recipes.

Roles: Product Designer, Product Designer, Prototyper
Technologies: Keynote, Sketch, Photoshop, 3D Printer & Laser Cutter

OH CRAB! (KINECT GAME)

Oh Crab! is a game that uses Kinect as input and is build around the story of a little crab that needs help to reach the ocean. The game is based on a novel mechanic that involves the juxtaposition of the body silhoutte above the game environment.

Roles: Game Designer, Sound Designer
Technologies: Adobe Audition, Unity, Paper Prototyping.

CONTACTIVE

Contactive is a startup based on NYC acquired by Fuze / ThinkingPhones. Contactive's main product is CallerID the leading inbound call identification solution for Android Devices with over a million downloads. As a Product Manager of Contactive I was in charge of two main projects: 1) Creating their first API product. 2) Designing an Outlook Plugin for Contactive.

Roles: Product Manager, Front-End Prototyper.
Technologies: HTML/CSS/JS, Keynote, Photoshop.
